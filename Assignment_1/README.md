# Global Food & Nutrition Database 2026

The Global Food & Nutrition Database 2026 is a high-fidelity, multimodal dataset designed for advanced nutritional analysis and healthcare AI. It synthesizes over 40,000 records from the USDA FoodData Central and Open Food Facts, uniquely bridging the gap between North American nutritional values and European health metrics.
